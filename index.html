Nerfies: Deformable Neural Radiance Fields

Authors

Matthew Tancik, Vincent Casser, Xinchen Yan, Sabeek Pradhan, Ben Mildenhall, Pratul P. Srinivasan, Jonathan T. Barron, Henrik Kretzschmar

Abstract

Nerfies is a method for modeling dynamic 3D scenes using neural radiance fields. It can handle complex deformations and captures high-quality details for rendering.
